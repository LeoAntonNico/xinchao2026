# THESIS — What We Believe

## Vision
Xin Chào is the best Vietnamese street food experience in the Netherlands. Two locations. One unified brand. Authentic flavors, modern convenience.

## Principles
1. **Authenticity first** — no fusion shortcuts. Phở is phở. Bún is bún.
2. **Speed matters** — online order to pickup in <15 min. Reservation booking in <3 clicks.
3. **Bilingual by default** — every customer sees Dutch first, English one click away.
4. **Capacity-based, not table-based** — we optimize throughput, not seating charts.
5. **Data-driven** — every decision runs through Rachel's numbers.

## Brand Voice
- Warm, not corporate
- Confident, not arrogant
- Vietnamese heritage + Dutch directness
- Dark, premium aesthetic (charcoal + gold + red)

## Tech Principles
- Next.js App Router, TypeScript, Tailwind v4
- PostgreSQL + Prisma — single source of truth
- Mollie iDEAL — Dutch payments done right
- NextAuth cookie sessions — simple, secure
- No third-party trackers without Rachel's approval
